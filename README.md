# notformelessnotvoid — AR Layer

Web AR experience for *Levels Algorithm 1*.  
Point your phone camera at the physical artwork to reveal the algorithmic facial mesh overlay.

## Live experience

**[laurimoyle.github.io/Levels-algorithm-1](https://laurimoyle.github.io/Levels-algorithm-1)**

Open this URL on your phone. Allow camera access. Point at the artwork.

## How it works

- Image tracking via [MindAR.js](https://hiukim.github.io/mind-ar-js-doc/)
- Facial landmark mesh (57 points, hand-mapped) rendered in Three.js
- Biometric measurement lines appear after ~4 seconds
- ANALYSIS COMPLETE — SUBJECT UNRESOLVED appears after ~15 seconds
- Requires HTTPS and camera permission

## Context

The work layers a hand-drawn 2D painted face with a hand-built 3D doodle pen structure.  
This AR layer adds what the algorithm thinks it sees — the cold geometry of facial recognition  
placed over what resists being reduced to geometry.

*Not formless. Not void.*
